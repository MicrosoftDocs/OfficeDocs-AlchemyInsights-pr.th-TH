[ลบการคืนค่าไซต์](https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/sharepoint/restore-deleted-site-collection)

[คืนค่าโดยใช้โมดูล SPO PowerShell](https://support.office.com/en-us/article/Introduction-to-the-SharePoint-Online-Management-Shell-C16941C3-19B4-4710-8056-34C034493429)
